OronokoPoker Website Proposal

  • About the Website
    • What is your website about? My website will be about all things Poker. Interesting hands, site recommendations, theory, discussion, and overall any interesting poker content on my mind.
    • What is the objective of your website? To grow my prescence in the Poker community and to network. I want to grow my twitch channel and find likeminded poker players whom I could do hand reviews and study with.
    • Who is your target audience set? My target audience is anyone interested in Poker.
  • What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?
    • Homepage- This will have all my information and credentials as a Poker player.
    • Recommendations- This is where I'll recommend software, poker websites, and optimal game type.
    • Forums- This is where users will have the option to interact, talk strategy, bad beats, or anything poker related that interests them.
    • Strategy Articles- This is where I will post in-depth strategic analysis of variety of poker sub-topics including three-betting, ICM, and post-flop theory.
    • Crazy Hand Log- This is where I'll put crazy, unbelievable hands that I have played.
